Nim Dorji Restaurant:App and Responsive Website

Project description

UX Case Study

Duration: 7th Aug - 30th Oct

Product Overview

Nim Dorji Restaurant offers a cutting-edge app and responsive website that revolutionizes the online dining experience by providing immersive 3D visualizations of dishes. Targeting tech-savvy diners and food enthusiasts, it ensures an engaging and informative meal selection process.


Problem

The current state of e-commerce websites lacks an interactive experience for customers, making it difficult to fully appreciate products through static 2D images.


Goal

Enhance the online dining experience by providing immersive and interactive 3D visualizations of dishes thropugh mobile app and responsive website.

User research: Summary

For Nim Dorji Restaurant's app and website, I conducted user research via surveys, interviews, and usability testing. Initially assuming customers prioritized convenience, I discovered they also seek engaging, visually rich experiences when browsing food items. This insight prompted me to focus on integrating 3D visualizations and interactive features to enhance user satisfaction.

Usability Study

Usability Study:Parameters

  • User Engagement: Compare the time spent on 3D images versus standard images to gauge interest.
  • Decision Confidence: Survey users on their confidence in purchases before and after using 3D images.
  • Conversion Rate: Analyze the increase in purchases or cart additions with 3D images available.

Usability Study:Findings

  • Increased Engagement: Users noted they spent more time interacting with 3D images compared to standard images.
  • Boosted Confidence: Users reported feeling more confident in their purchasing decisions after using 3D images.
  • Higher Conversions: Users indicated they were more likely to add items to their cart and complete purchases when 3D images were available.

Accessibility

Text Descriptions

  • Provide detailed text descriptions for each food item alongside 3D images for better accessibility.

    • High Contrast Mode

  • Implement a high contrast mode for the web app to make 3D images and text easier to see for users with visual impairments.

    • Simplified Navigation

  • Design a simplified navigation mode that highlights essential features and food items, making it easier for users with cognitive disabilities to make decisions.
